Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

SecureHome+ ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you register, we collect your name and email address via Firebase Authentication.

Location Data: We request access to your device's location to provide nearby safety points (Police/Fire stations) and to include your coordinates in Emergency Alerts. This data is only processed when the app is in use or when an alert is triggered.

SMS Data: Our app uses the SMS permission solely to send emergency alerts to your designated family members. We do not read your private messages.

AI Interactions: When you chat with "Health Gini" (powered by Google Gemini), the text of your queries is processed to provide safety and health advice.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core security features (Home Surveys, Emergency Alerts).
  • To display nearby safety resources on the map.
  • To manage your family emergency contact list.
  • To provide AI-driven health and security advice via Health Gini.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own policies:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and data storage.
  • Google Gemini AI: For processing AI assistant queries.
  • OpenStreetMap (osmdroid): For map rendering.

4. Data Retention and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase. You can request to delete your account and associated data at any time through the "My Account" section in the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@securehomeplus.com
